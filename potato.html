Potato Fight is a third-person shooter developed by oh my cheese games. The game features anthromorphized potatoes, who battle each other using butter to "butter" enemies. It focuses on multiplayer battles, in which eight players face off in a struggle for "butter points". Players can use secondary weapons and special weapons to defeat enemies and can swim through gravy for sneak attacks. It is currently planned for a june 2017 release.
